Activities

  • Home
  • Academics
  • Activites

The school complements the ICSE board curriculum with the addition of extracurricular and co-curricular activities for the students to ensure their holistic growth. Extra-curricular activities are outside-of-the-classroom activities not tied up with the educational curriculum. Co-curricular activities are those which are beyond but complementary to the regular curriculum.

  • Extra-Curricular Activities
  • Co-Curricular Activities
  • School Houses
Dance

As an extra-curricular activity, Dance is mandatory for students to train themselves in increasing muscular strength, motor fitness, and endurance.

Yogasan

Yogasan is compulsory for the students to learn stress management, and take care of physical, and mental health.

Arts and crafts

The class of arts and crafts where students bring out their paint brushes, colors, canvases, scissors, chisels, papers, and other craft materials to create new things. Engaging in arts and crafts activity actually helps students to give the shape of their idea that supports increasing creativity.

Music

Music classes help students with their brain development following through assistance with sound recognition, and teach rhythm and rhyme. Songs help children to remember information.

Quizzes

Quiz is one of the essential co-curricular activities which influence students to read the out of syllabus material and strengthen their knowledge.

Educational Subjective Competitions

Students engage in educational subjective competition to support students in building STEM skills.

Science Olympiads

Students engage in an olympiad that helps them for developing a set of skills and improve their aptitude. Engaging students in the olympiad results in improving academic performance.

Extempores

Students engage in extempore encouraging them to think creatively and quickly. It helps to practice one’s communication and time management skills.

Project Exhibitions

Project exhibitions help students to learn new concepts and develop core knowledge about the subjects.

Debates

Classroom debates help students learn through friendly competition, promoting problem-solving and innovative thinking.

About School

Mother Teresa Lions School is an initiative from the Lions Club Durgachak. The school was established in 2008 and is located in Durgachak under Purba Medinipur district, West Bengal. The school aims to provide quality education to students.
