The curriculum that supports planning the whole integrated education process including time, period and sessions, and others. In the education process, the curriculum comprises a continuous chain of meaningful activities which aims to enable teachers and students as well for achieving the educational goal. The school is affiliated with the ICSE board of education. The school curriculum follows the ICSE board curriculum. The ICSE curriculum teaches through the English medium of teaching. The board council focuses on the holistic growth of the student by providing a balanced structure to the learner.

About School

Mother Teresa Lions School is an initiative from the Lions Club Durgachak. The school was established in 2008 and is located in Durgachak under Purba Medinipur district, West Bengal. The school aims to provide quality education to students.
