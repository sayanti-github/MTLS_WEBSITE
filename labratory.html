Library

A well-established library system is available to motivate students for learning new things and reading. Major rules and regulations for using the library have mentioned below-

  • Books will be issued to students from Grade III and upper grades.
  • Each student is allowed to issue one book at a time for one week.
  • No books will be issued further if not submitted within the return timeline.
  • Fines will be imposed on the borrower if the allotted book is damaged, disfigured, or damaged.
  • Only textbooks and library books are allowed to be read on the school premises.

About School

Mother Teresa Lions School is an initiative from the Lions Club Durgachak. The school was established in 2008 and is located in Durgachak under Purba Medinipur district, West Bengal. The school aims to provide quality education to students.
